This game displays a single question at a time until the player answers it or their time runs out. If the player selects the correct answer, a message pops up congratulating them for choosing the right option. After a few seconds, the next question displays. The scenario is similar for wrong answers and time-outs. Each question also has a "bad" answer that generates a unique message! If the player runs out of time, there is a message that time's up. On the final screen, the player sees the number of correct answers, incorrect answers, and an option to restart the game.
